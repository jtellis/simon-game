How to play

Simon will display the first color in the sequence. You must then press the displayed color. Simon will then repeat the first color and then display another. You must press these two displayed colors in order. Simon will continue by displaying the first two colors in order followed by another color, and you must repeat the sequence. Simon will continue adding to the sequence for as long as you can go! Simon will also speed up the longer you keep up! You can also select Easy, Medium or Hard difficulty, which will set the overall speed of the sequence. Good luck!
